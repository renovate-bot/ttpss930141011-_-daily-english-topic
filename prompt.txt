## Link:

Provide a relevant Reddit thread link as today's discussion topic.

## 1. Topic Introduction:

Briefly summarize the Reddit thread and highlight its relevance to English learners.

## 2. Learning Points:

### 2.1 Common Colloquial Phrases & Idioms:

List and explain useful everyday phrases and idioms found in the topic.

### 2.2 Abbreviations & Internet Slang:

Include commonly used internet abbreviations and explain their meaning clearly.

### 2.3 Key Grammar & Sentence Patterns:

Introduce and exemplify one or two essential grammar points directly related to today's topic.

## 3. Pronunciation & Intonation Practice:

Suggest phrases from the learning points for pronunciation and intonation practice.

## 4. Open-Ended Discussion Questions:

Provide two thought-provoking, open-ended questions that encourage learners to reflect and discuss in English.

## 5. Template Answers (Conversational English):

Provide two example answers in natural, conversational English that learners can emulate.

## 6. Cultural Background or Context Explanation:

Include a brief explanation of relevant cultural context or background information related to the topic to enhance learners' understanding.

## 7. Extension Activity:

Suggest a brief, practical activity that learners can complete to apply today's learning points actively.

Ensure all content is clear, concise, and tailored specifically for English learners at intermediate to advanced levels.
